05 — Motion & Animations

Fade Up
Elements enter from 30px below, opacity 0→1. Duration: 0.7s ease. Used on section reveals and card entrances.
animation: fadeUp 0.7s ease both
Slide In Left
Content slides from -40px, opacity 0→1. Duration: 0.7s ease. Used for text blocks alongside images.
animation: slideInLeft 0.7s ease both
Scale In
Cards scale from 0.94, opacity 0→1. Duration: 0.6s ease. Used for card and modal entrances.
animation: scaleIn 0.6s ease both
Bounce Loop
Scroll indicator bounces 8px down infinitely. Duration: 1.4s ease-in-out. Used on hero scroll prompt.
animation: bounceDown 1.4s ease-in-out infinite
Grayscale Reveal
Hover on neighborhood cards removes grayscale and slightly brightens. Duration: 0.5s ease.
filter: grayscale(0) 0.5s ease
Transition Timing
All interactive transitions: 0.3s ease. Image hovers: 0.5s ease. Easing always ease or ease-in-out — never linear.
transition: all 0.3s ease
