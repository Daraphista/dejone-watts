    About 2 Rivers Team

    Who we are

    The 2 Rivers Team was inspired by the mighty Potomac and Anacostia rivers that flow through DC, Maryland, and Virginia (the DMV). Connecting buyers and sellers in all three jurisdictions is what we do and we are proudly a multi-year awarded Washingtonian Best of Team. We know that real estate is both emotional and analytical, and that's why we've got both heart and smarts.

    We represent clients from all walks of life and every stage of living. Whether you're a first-time buyer, downsizing, or looking to invest, we've got you covered. With our extensive and deep local knowledge of the real estate market, we'll guide you through every step of the way.
